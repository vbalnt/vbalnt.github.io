I am currently Research Team Lead at Scape Technologies, where our team focuses on researching problems related to large-scale localisation.

Previously I was a PhD student at Imperial College London and University of Surrey, working with Krystian Mikolajczyk, and a post-doctoral researcher at the Active Vision Lab, University of Oxford working with Victor Prisacariu. I am also a co-organiser of the London Computer Vision Meetup.
